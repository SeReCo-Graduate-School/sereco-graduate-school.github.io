# SeReCo Workshop 2026

We invite you to join the Workshop 2026 of the Doctoral College for Semantics, Reasoning and Coordination.

The Workshop will be hosted on 18th and 19th of March 2026 in Nuremberg, Germany, and is open to all PhD students and researchers of the participating organizations:

- Ecole des Mines Saint-Étienne
- Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg
- Université Jean Monnet Saint-Étienne
- Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)
- Universität St. Gallen

## Location

The workshop is located at the <a target="_blank" href="https://www.wiso.rw.fau.de/">Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg School of Business, Economics and Society</a>  **Lange Gasse 20** in **90403 Nuremberg, Germany**, see <a target="_blank" href="https://www.wiso.rw.fau.eu/map-and-directions/">map and directions</a>.
